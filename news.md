# Daily AI News

We curate the latest AI news by aggregating content from trusted RSS feeds across the AI ecosystem every day. Our automated pipeline monitors multiple sources to ensure you stay informed about the most recent developments in artificial intelligence.

## How It Works

Our news aggregation system continuously monitors RSS feeds from leading AI publications, research labs, and tech news outlets. Each article is automatically categorized and tagged for easy discovery.

## News Archive

All daily news entries are archived in the `feed-entries` directory, organized by date. Each day's AI news is stored in a dedicated folder named with the date format `YYYY-MM-DD`, with individual articles saved as markdown files prefixed with the publication timestamp.

The archive is automatically sorted in reverse chronological order, putting the most recent news at the top of the sidebar for easy access.

## Content Sources

We pull AI-related content from a variety of sources including:

- **AI Research Labs** - Latest papers, announcements, and breakthroughs from major AI organizations
- **Tech News** - Industry updates, funding news, and product launches
- **Developer Communities** - Coding tutorials, framework updates, and open source projects
- **AI Ethics & Policy** - Regulatory updates, safety research, and responsible AI discussions

## Update Frequency

New articles are added daily, ensuring you always have access to the freshest AI content. All entries are timestamped with their publication date and organized chronologically in the sidebar.
