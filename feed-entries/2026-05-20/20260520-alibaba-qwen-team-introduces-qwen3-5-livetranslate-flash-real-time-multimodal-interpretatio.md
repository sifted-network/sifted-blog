<p>Alibaba's Qwen team has released Qwen3.5-LiveTranslate-Flash, a real-time multimodal translation model that processes audio and video simultaneously. The model covers 60 input languages and produces speech output in 29 languages at 2.8 seconds of latency. Key additions over the previous Qwen3 version include real-time speaker voice cloning, vision-enhanced comprehension via lip movements and on-screen text, and dynamic keyword configuration for domain-specific terminology. On FLEURS and CoVoST2 benchmarks, the model outperforms major commercial alternatives. It is available as an API-only model through Alibaba Cloud Model Studio using a WebSocket-based protocol.</p>
